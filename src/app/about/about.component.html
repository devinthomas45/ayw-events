About the AYW

Our Company

"As You Wish, LLC , based in San Antonio, Texas, started out like any other business, a quick and small idea that we could do it better. After 5 years of back and forth and trying to figure out what to call the company and what we actually wanted to do, the company was finally born in September of 2018. During a late night movie with friends, Gabby came into the room and said I did it, I started the process of making the business. I don't have to explain how many jaws dropped at the moment, 3 friends who have been dreaming about this for years, was finally here. The hard task and long process of now actually starting a small business was now under way.

With a combined experience of 40 years in the hospitality industry, each of us knows what it means to provide nothing but the best expereince for our clients and to build only the best memories.

AYW aims to provide almost a 'One Stop Shop' for event needs, having available services such as Photography, Catering, Event Services, and more. As we begin to build and grow, we always remember that our main goal is the satisfaction of our client and their guests.

As You Wish, LLC strives to provide amazing service regarless of race, gender, nationality, sexual orientation, and all of the above. Strong supporters of the LGBTQIA+ community, we want each client who utilizes our service(s) to feel comfortable and confident that privacy, safety, and their overall environment is respected."
